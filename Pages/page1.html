Chocolate Chip Cookies

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

- Ingredients -

- - - - - - - - - -

  • 125g Unsalted Butter
  • 50g Brown Sugar
  • 50g White Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 225g Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 100g Choclate Chips
  • Pinch of Salt

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

- Instructions -

- - - - - - - - - -

  1. Preheat the oven to 170 Degree Celcius. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Beat the butter and both types of sugar together using a whisk. Add the egg and vanilla extract, then continue whisking untill all combined.
  3. Sift in the flour, baking powder, and salt. Combine all ingredients.
  4. Divide the mixture into 24 balls of dough and arrange them on the prepared baking trays, making sure to leave apporiate space between them.
  5. Bake for about

- Enjoy! -
